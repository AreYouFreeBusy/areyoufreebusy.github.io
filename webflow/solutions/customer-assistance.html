How customers can schedule time to meet with a customer support team member

Through FreeBusy, the customers can schedule one-on-one or group calls, training sessions, webinars or product demos.

1. Share your availability

Customers ca easily schedule time to chat with you by checking your availability in real-time (individual or team availability).

2. Customers will select dates and times to chat

You or your support team will receive an email with a link to view the meeting on their calendars, with all the meeting details.
